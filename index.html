⚠ IDF Claim vs Full Picture

IDF's cited figure of "~300 launchers destroyed" refers specifically to ballistic missile launchers (LRBM/MRBM/SRBM + fixed sites = ~385 total pre-war). Of these, ~260 are estimated destroyed (~68%). Drone launch/control systems and cruise missile platforms are harder to locate and most survive. Remaining ballistic launchers are dispersed into tunnels, civilian areas, and mountain terrain, making further strikes progressively more difficult.
